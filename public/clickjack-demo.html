Clickjacking Demonstration

Lab Note: This page demonstrates how an attacker can overlay a "Fake" UI over your application. Currently, the target app below is at 30% opacity to show you what's happening. In a real attack, it would be 0%.

In this scenario, the attacker baits you to click "Claim Reward", but your click is actually hitting a sensitive button in the application hidden underneath (like "Delete Account" or "Confirm Purchase").
